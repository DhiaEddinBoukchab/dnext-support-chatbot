Thank you for your response. Here is the contract revised following your legal review.
We are aligned with all the points except the clause regarding termination with 30 days’ notice.

Unfortunately, we are unable to offer this option to any client.

Why?
From day one, we provide full access to our entire database, including historical data that has taken many years to build. This data represents substantial value and is a core asset of our company. Allowing termination on short notice would expose us to a significant risk, as a client could benefit immediately from all our data and end the collaboration shortly after.

What is your risk?
You receive the service and, if at the end of the year you are not fully satisfied, you simply choose not to renew. You still keep the full historical database and your only cost is the annual fee of 50k.

What is our risk?
We deliver everything from day one. If you are not satisfied or decide not to renew for any reason, we bear the full risk.

For these reasons, we maintain a minimum 12-month commitment for all clients. We remain available to discuss any remaining points and finalise the agreement. When ready for execution, we will modify the start date as Dec 1st.
