The issue regarding Export License vs. FS has been resolved.
Regarding the corn data not being updated since November, I reviewed the data pipelines and found that the crop year (season) is calculated in Snowflake. As shown in the code below, the crop year is derived from the shipment_date_from column (FECHA DE INICIO PER in the source):

*	
	if the month is strictly greater than February, the crop year corresponds to the calendar year
*	
	otherwise, it is calculated as year − 1.

After reviewing both the source data and the datasets, the only records that meet this condition are those displayed in the screenshots below (24 Sept, 25 Sept and 7 Nov ) and the cumulated sum is the same in the source, dataset, and dashboard 305,000
