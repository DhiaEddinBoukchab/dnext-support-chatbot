**Problem**: The customer requested the setup of Dnext accounts for multiple users, specifically asking for login details to be sent to them.

**Context**: The user is a Senior Trader from a Customer Organization requesting account setups for colleagues. The product involved is Dnext accounts, and the request was made via email to a Data Analyst at the Service Provider.
