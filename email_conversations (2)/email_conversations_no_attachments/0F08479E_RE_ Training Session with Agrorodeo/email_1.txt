Following up on your inquiry about comparing actual precipitation with forecast values in the weather dashboards, here’s a step-by-step guide:

 

1.	Open the Weather Dashboards

	a.	Go to Dashboard > Weather, then select the region you want to review.

 

2.	Access the Comparison Tool

	a.	Click the “Forecast Run Comparison” button. This will take you to the comparison page.

 



 

3.	Choose What to Compare

	a.	In the “Run Selection” filter:

                                                                              i.       Check the box for “Actual” to display observed precipitation.

                                                                           ii.        Select the forecast model(s) you want to compare against.

 



 

 

4.	View the Results

	b.	Hover your mouse over the chart to see the actual precipitation values versus the forecast runs for the chosen date.

 



 

I hope this helps you find what you are looking for.
