**Problem**: The customer needed login credentials for various Dnext environments to access the system.

**Context**: The user is a new member of the Customer Support team at a Customer Organization. The request involved accessing the Production, Development, and Staging environments of the Dnext platform.

**Solution**: The Support Agent provided the customer with the necessary login credentials for each environment and instructed them to change their password upon first login. The customer was advised to follow specific steps to update their password for security purposes.
