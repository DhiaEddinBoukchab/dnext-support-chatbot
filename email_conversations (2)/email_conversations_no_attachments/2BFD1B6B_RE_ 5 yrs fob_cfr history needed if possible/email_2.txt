Hello Ruben.

 

I hope this message finds you well.

 

You can find the data you are looking for on the platform by following the instructions below : 

 

For Corn : 

 

1.	First Login to the platform then go to the datasets by navigation to the left menu and hovering over the table icon then clicking on dataset
2.	You should land on a table containing the datasets available on your environment
3.	In the name field of the table search for ‘Corn’, then look for the dataset with the name : “World Corn CNF Updates”
4.	Click on the Eye icon on the right of the row 
5.	You should land on the dataset summary page where you will see a “Download”Button on the top right of the page , click on that button and the data should be downloaded

Note : see screenshots below for visual aid.

 





 

Soybean : 

 

1.	First Login to the platform then go to the datasets by navigation to the left menu and hovering over the table icon then clicking on dataset
2.	You should land on a table containing the datasets available on your environment
3.	In the name field of the table search for ‘Soybean’, then look for the dataset with the name : “dnexr Soybean WORLD UNS daily”
4.	Click on the Eye icon on the right of the row 
5.	You should land on the dataset summary page where you will see a “Download”Button on the top right of the page , click on that button and the data should be downloaded

Note : see screenshots below for visual aid.
