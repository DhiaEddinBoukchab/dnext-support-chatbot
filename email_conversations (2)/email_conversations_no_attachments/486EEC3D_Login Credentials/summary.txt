**Problem**: The customer needed assistance with accessing the platform using provided login credentials.

**Context**: The user is an external user from a Customer Organization attempting to log into a platform provided by a Service Provider. The email includes specific login credentials and instructions for changing the password upon first login.

**Solution**: The Support Agent provided the customer with the login URL and credentials, along with detailed instructions for changing the password after the initial login. It is recommended that the user change their password immediately after logging in for security purposes.
