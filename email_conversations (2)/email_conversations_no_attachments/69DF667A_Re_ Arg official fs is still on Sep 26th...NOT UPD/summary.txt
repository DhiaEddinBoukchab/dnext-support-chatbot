**Problem**: The customer reported that the official financial statement for Arg was still showing the September 26th data and had not been updated.

**Context**: The user is an external user from Customer Organization, inquiring about a delay in data updates for a financial reporting feature provided by Service Provider. The user was informed that there is typically a one-week delay from the source for data updates.

**Solution**: The issue was resolved by confirming that the data update had occurred and that the dashboard refresh would be initiated. It was recommended that users be aware of the typical one-week delay for future updates to manage expectations.
