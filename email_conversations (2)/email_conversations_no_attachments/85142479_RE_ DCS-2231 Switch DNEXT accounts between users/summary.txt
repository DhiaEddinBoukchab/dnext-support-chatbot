**Problem**: The customer requested to add three new users to the distribution lists for reports after switching DNEXT accounts between users.

**Context**: The customer is an external user from a global purchasing role at a Customer Organization. The request involves the DNEXT platform, specifically regarding user account management and report distribution.

**Solution**: The Support Agent confirmed the removal of old user accounts and the creation of new accounts for the requested users.
