To download the data, navigate to Dashboards Module 



 

Then go to the Crop Tab look for Brazil Expand the button and look for Crop Progress 

 



 

Once there you will see a report choose IMEA :

 



 

Then hover your mouse on the matrix and a menu will show up click on the three dots and choose Export Data 

 



 

Choose “Underlying Data” option and click Export 

 



 

Also you can download the raw data used to create the report from the Datasets Module : 

 



 

Here are the four datasets for IMEA :
