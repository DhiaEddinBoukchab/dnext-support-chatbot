“How can I see below info in the system (for example I am looking for soybean meal exports by month from the us?”

 



 

You can find this info by navigating to the dashboards Module then Supply and Demand > DNEXR > Oilseeds 

See image Below for visual aid

 



 

You can find the monthly export data for Soybean Meals from the U.S. in the SBM SBS TradeMatrix. To access it:

1.	Go to the "TradeMatrixes" module.
2.	Look for the row labeled "Soybean and Soybean Meal" in the column name.
3.	To the right of that row, click on the eye icon to view the TradeMatrix.

 



 

4.	Once inside, you can filter by origin (USA) and product (Soybean Meal) to see the monthly export data.
5.	You can download a more detailed Excel table by clicking the download button in the top right corner of the screen. Additionally, to download an Excel sheet with the data as displayed in the TradeMatrix, right-click on the bottom half of the table, hover over "Export," and then choose to download either a CSV or Excel file.(see image below for visual aid)
