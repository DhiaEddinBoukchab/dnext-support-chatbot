**Problem**: The customer requested access to the Viserion Snowflake database for themselves and a colleague.

**Context**: The user is a Data Analyst from Customer Organization. They reached out to the Customer Support team of the Service Provider for assistance in gaining access to a specific database.

**Solution**: The Support Agent created a ticket for the development team to address the access request. The customer was informed that they would be updated once there is feedback from the development team regarding the access.
