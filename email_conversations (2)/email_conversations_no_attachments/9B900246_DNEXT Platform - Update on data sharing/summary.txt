**Problem**: The customer is inquiring about the upcoming changes to the DNEXT platform regarding automated access to datasets and tradeflows based on their subscription.

**Context**: The customer is an external user of the DNEXT platform, which is a data service provider. The email outlines enhancements aimed at improving user experience by automating data sharing and updating resource codes.
