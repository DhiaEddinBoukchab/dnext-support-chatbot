**Problem**: The customer mistakenly used an incorrect email address to send documents, leading to confusion and a request for clarification on the assistance needed.

**Context**: The user is an external user from a Customer Organization communicating with a Support Agent from a Service Provider. The issue arose during an email exchange regarding a document update related to a specific project.

**Solution**: The customer acknowledged the mistake and resent the emails to the correct internal storage for easy retrieval. The Support Agent requested additional context to assist further, ensuring that the customer could clarify their needs moving forward. It is recommended to double-check email addresses before sending important communications to avoid similar issues.
